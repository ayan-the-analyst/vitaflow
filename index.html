Why Monitor from the Neck?

The neck provides direct access to the blood vessels supplying your brain, offering more accurate data than wrist-based devices.

Direct Vascular Access

Monitors the carotid artery and jugular vein for precise cerebral blood flow measurements.

Central Pressure Reading

Provides central blood pressure data that correlates directly with brain perfusion.

Minimal Signal Interference

Less affected by motion artifacts compared to wrist-based sensors during daily activities.

Advanced Health Monitoring Features

Cerebral Blood Flow
